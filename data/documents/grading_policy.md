# Technify University — Grading Policy

## 1. Grade Scale

| Grade | Percentage Range | Grade Points | Interpretation |
|-------|-----------------|-------------|----------------|
| A+ | 90 - 100% | 4.00 | Exceptional |
| A | 85 - 89% | 4.00 | Excellent |
| A- | 80 - 84% | 3.67 | Very Good |
| B+ | 75 - 79% | 3.33 | Good |
| B | 70 - 74% | 3.00 | Above Average |
| B- | 65 - 69% | 2.67 | Average |
| C+ | 60 - 64% | 2.33 | Below Average |
| C | 55 - 59% | 2.00 | Satisfactory |
| C- | 50 - 54% | 1.67 | Minimum Pass |
| D | 45 - 49% | 1.00 | Conditional Pass |
| F | Below 45% | 0.00 | Fail |

## 2. Special Grades

| Grade | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| W | Withdrawn (student dropped the course before deadline) |
| I | Incomplete (student has pending requirements) |
| P | Pass (for pass/fail courses only) |
| NP | Not Pass (for pass/fail courses only) |
| IP | In Progress (for multi-semester courses) |

## 3. Assessment Breakdown

The standard assessment breakdown is:

| Component | Weight | Details |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| Quizzes | 15% | 3-4 quizzes, best 3 counted |
| Assignments | 10% | 2-3 assignments |
| Midterm Exam | 25% | Written exam in Week 8 |
| Final Exam | 50% | Written exam at end of semester |

Note: Lab courses may have a different breakdown as determined by the instructor.

## 4. GPA Calculation

### Semester GPA
Semester GPA = Sum of (Course Grade Points x Credit Hours) / Total Credit Hours for the semester

### Cumulative GPA (CGPA)
CGPA = Sum of (All Course Grade Points x Credit Hours) / Total Credit Hours completed

### Example Calculation:

| Course | Credits | Grade | Grade Points | Quality Points |
|--------|---------|-------|-------------|---------------|
| Data Structures | 3 | A | 4.00 | 12.00 |
| Web Engineering | 3 | B+ | 3.33 | 9.99 |
| Database Systems | 3 | B | 3.00 | 9.00 |
| English II | 3 | A- | 3.67 | 11.01 |
| Physics II | 3 | C+ | 2.33 | 6.99 |
| **Total** | **15** | | | **48.99** |

Semester GPA = 48.99 / 15 = **3.27** (Good Standing)

## 5. Academic Standing

| CGPA Range | Standing | Action |
|-----------|---------|--------|
| 3.50 - 4.00 | Dean's Honor List | Certificate of recognition |
| 3.00 - 3.49 | Good Standing | None |
| 2.50 - 2.99 | Satisfactory | None |
| 2.00 - 2.49 | Probation Warning | Academic advisor meeting required |
| Below 2.00 | Academic Probation | Must improve in next semester or face dismissal |

## 6. Probation Rules

Students on Academic Probation:
- Must take minimum course load (12 credits)
- Cannot take more than 15 credits
- Must attend weekly meetings with academic advisor
- Must achieve semester GPA of at least 2.50 to exit probation
- If CGPA remains below 2.00 for 2 consecutive semesters: **Dismissal**

## 7. Grade Change Policy

- Grades are finalized within **7 days** of result publication.
- After finalization, grades can only change through:
  1. Re-checking appeal (within 7 days, PKR 500 fee)
  2. Error correction by instructor (with department head approval)
- Grade changes require approval from: Instructor + Department Head + Dean
