# Technify University — Student Code of Conduct

## 1. General Conduct

All students of Technify University are expected to:
- Maintain professional and respectful behavior on campus.
- Respect fellow students, faculty, and staff regardless of background, gender, or beliefs.
- Follow all university policies and regulations.
- Uphold academic integrity in all academic work.
- Dress appropriately as per university dress code.

## 2. Dress Code

- Students must wear their **University ID Card** at all times on campus.
- Clothing must be **decent and appropriate** for an academic setting.
- Slippers, shorts, and sleeveless shirts are **not allowed** for male students.
- Lab coats are mandatory in all **laboratory sessions**.

## 3. Academic Integrity

### Plagiarism
- Submitting someone else's work as your own is strictly prohibited.
- All assignments are checked through **plagiarism detection software**.
- Maximum allowed similarity: **20%**.
- Penalty: Zero in the assignment (first offense), F in course (second offense).

### Cheating
- Any form of cheating in exams or assessments is a serious offense.
- See Examination Rules for detailed penalties.

## 4. Prohibited Activities

The following are strictly prohibited on campus:
- Smoking (campus is a smoke-free zone)
- Possession or use of drugs or alcohol
- Bullying, harassment, or ragging of any kind
- Damage to university property
- Unauthorized recording of lectures without permission
- Political activities or protests without prior approval
- Use of mobile phones during lectures

## 5. Disciplinary Process

### Step 1: Warning
- First minor offense results in a verbal warning from the instructor or department.

### Step 2: Written Warning
- Second offense or moderate violation results in a written warning letter.
- Letter is placed in the student's permanent file.

### Step 3: Disciplinary Committee
- Serious or repeated violations are referred to the Disciplinary Committee.
- The student has the right to present their case.
- Possible outcomes: suspension, community service, fine, or expulsion.

### Step 4: Appeal
- Students may appeal the committee's decision to the Vice Chancellor within 14 days.
- The Vice Chancellor's decision is final.

## 6. Use of IT Resources

- University internet is for **academic purposes only**.
- Downloading pirated software, movies, or games is prohibited.
- Accessing inappropriate websites on campus network is monitored and prohibited.
- Hacking or attempting to access unauthorized systems is a **criminal offense**.
- Students must not share their login credentials with others.

## 7. Library Rules

- Silence must be maintained in the library at all times.
- Books can be borrowed for **14 days** (renewable once).
- Late return fine: **PKR 50 per day**.
- Lost books must be replaced or paid for at **double the book price**.
- Food and drinks are not allowed in the library.

## 8. Complaints and Grievances

Students may file complaints through:
- **Online portal:** erp.technify.edu.pk/complaints
- **Email:** studentaffairs@technify.edu.pk
- **In person:** Student Affairs Office (Block A, Room 101)

All complaints are confidential and will be addressed within **7 working days**.
