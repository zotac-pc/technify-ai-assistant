# Technify University — Examination Rules and Regulations

## 1. Types of Examinations

| Exam Type | Weight | When |
|-----------|--------|------|
| Quizzes (3-4) | 15% | Throughout the semester |
| Assignments (2-3) | 10% | As per instructor |
| Midterm Exam | 25% | Week 8 of the semester |
| Final Exam | 50% | Last 2 weeks of semester |

## 2. Eligibility for Final Exam

A student is eligible to appear in the final examination ONLY if:
- They have a minimum **75% attendance** in the course.
- They have attempted the **midterm examination**.
- They have no outstanding **fee dues**.
- They have no **disciplinary holds** on their account.

## 3. Exam Conduct Rules

### Before the Exam:
- Students must arrive **15 minutes** before the scheduled exam time.
- Students must carry their **University ID card** — No ID, No Entry.
- Electronic devices (mobile phones, smartwatches) must be **switched off** and placed in bags.

### During the Exam:
- No communication with other students is allowed.
- Only authorized materials (as specified by the instructor) are permitted.
- Students may **NOT leave the exam hall** during the first 30 minutes or last 15 minutes.
- Bathroom breaks are allowed only with **invigilator escort** (one student at a time).

### After the Exam:
- Answer sheets must be submitted to the invigilator.
- Students must leave the exam hall quietly.

## 4. Cheating and Unfair Means

The following constitute cheating:
- Copying from another student's answer sheet
- Using unauthorized materials (cheat sheets, mobile phones)
- Communicating with other students during the exam
- Submitting someone else's work as your own (plagiarism)

### Penalties:
| Offense | Penalty |
|---------|---------|
| First offense | **Zero in that exam** + warning letter |
| Second offense | **F grade in the course** + disciplinary hearing |
| Third offense | **Suspension for one semester** |

## 5. Re-Examination Policy

- Students who miss an exam due to **medical emergency** may apply for a re-exam.
- Medical certificate must be submitted within **48 hours**.
- Re-exams are held within **2 weeks** of the original exam date.
- Maximum marks available in a re-exam is **80%** of the total.

## 6. Grade Appeals

- Students may appeal their grades within **7 days** of result publication.
- Appeal fee: **PKR 500** per course (refundable if grade changes).
- The paper will be re-checked by a **committee of two faculty members**.
- The committee's decision is **final**.

## 7. Grading Scale

| Grade | Percentage | Grade Points |
|-------|-----------|-------------|
| A+ | 90-100% | 4.00 |
| A | 85-89% | 4.00 |
| A- | 80-84% | 3.67 |
| B+ | 75-79% | 3.33 |
| B | 70-74% | 3.00 |
| B- | 65-69% | 2.67 |
| C+ | 60-64% | 2.33 |
| C | 55-59% | 2.00 |
| C- | 50-54% | 1.67 |
| D | 45-49% | 1.00 |
| F | Below 45% | 0.00 |

## 8. CGPA Calculation

CGPA = Sum of (Grade Points x Credit Hours) / Total Credit Hours

### Academic Standing:
| CGPA | Standing |
|------|---------|
| 3.50 - 4.00 | Dean's Honor List |
| 3.00 - 3.49 | Good Standing |
| 2.50 - 2.99 | Satisfactory |
| 2.00 - 2.49 | Probation Warning |
| Below 2.00 | Academic Probation |
