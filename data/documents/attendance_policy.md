# Technify University — Attendance Policy

## 1. Minimum Attendance Requirement

All students enrolled at Technify University are required to maintain a minimum attendance of **75%** in each registered course during the semester.

## 2. Attendance Tracking

- Attendance is recorded by the course instructor at the beginning of each class.
- Students are marked as **Present**, **Absent**, or **Late**.
- Two (2) **Late** markings are equivalent to one (1) **Absent** marking.

## 3. Consequences of Low Attendance

### Below 75% Attendance:
- The student will **not be allowed to sit in the final examination** for that course.
- The student will receive an **"F" grade** automatically.
- An official warning letter will be sent to the student and their guardian.

### Below 50% Attendance:
- The student will be automatically **dropped from the course**.
- The course will appear as **"W" (Withdrawn)** on the transcript.

## 4. Exemptions

The following are valid reasons for attendance exemptions:
- **Medical emergencies** (with a medical certificate from a recognized hospital)
- **University-approved events** (sports, academic competitions, official trips)
- **Bereavement** (death of an immediate family member — up to 5 days)

Exemption requests must be submitted to the Department Head within **7 days** of the absence.

## 5. Make-Up Classes

- If a class is cancelled by the instructor, a make-up class will be scheduled.
- Attendance in make-up classes is **mandatory**.
- Make-up classes are usually held on Saturdays.

## 6. Online/Hybrid Classes

- For online classes, attendance is tracked via the university LMS login.
- Students must be present for at least **80%** of the online session duration.
- Logging in and immediately leaving is considered as **Absent**.

## 7. Appeals

Students who believe their attendance has been recorded incorrectly may submit an appeal to the Department Head within **14 days** of the discrepancy. The appeal must include:
- Student ID and course details
- Specific dates in question
- Supporting evidence (e.g., LMS logs, instructor confirmation)
