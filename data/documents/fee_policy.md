## 2. Payment Schedule

| Installment | Deadline | Amount |
|------------|----------|--------|
| 1st Installment | Before semester starts | 50% of total fee |
| 2nd Installment | Week 4 of semester | 30% of total fee |
| 3rd Installment | Week 8 of semester | 20% of total fee |

## 3. Late Payment Penalties

| Days Late | Fine |
|-----------|------|
| 1-7 days | PKR 1,000 |
| 8-14 days | PKR 2,500 |
| 15-30 days | PKR 5,000 |
| Over 30 days | Account frozen + PKR 5,000 |

**Account Frozen** means:
- Cannot access exam results
- Cannot register for next semester
- Cannot appear in final exams
- Cannot receive degree/transcript

## 4. Scholarships and Financial Aid

### Merit Scholarships

| CGPA | Discount |
|------|---------|
| 3.90 - 4.00 | 100% tuition waiver |
| 3.70 - 3.89 | 75% tuition waiver |
| 3.50 - 3.69 | 50% tuition waiver |
| 3.30 - 3.49 | 25% tuition waiver |

### Need-Based Financial Aid
- Students from low-income families may apply for financial aid.
- Application deadline: 2 weeks before semester starts.
- Required documents: Family income certificate, utility bills, CNIC copies.

### Sibling Discount
- 2nd sibling: 10% discount
- 3rd sibling onwards: 15% discount

## 5. Refund Policy

| When | Refund |
|------|--------|
| Before classes start | 100% refund (minus admin fee) |
| Within first 2 weeks | 80% refund |
| Within first 4 weeks | 50% refund |
| After 4 weeks | No refund |

## 6. Payment Methods

- Bank transfer (HBL, UBL, Meezan Bank)
- Online payment via university portal
- Cash at university cashier (office hours only)
- JazzCash / EasyPaisa (convenience fee applies)
