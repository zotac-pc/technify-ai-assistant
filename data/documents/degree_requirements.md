## 2. Required Course Categories

### General Education (18-22 Credits)
- English I & II (6 credits)
- Islamic Studies / Ethics (3 credits)
- Pakistan Studies (3 credits)
- Communication Skills (3 credits)
- Calculus I & II (6 credits)

### Core Courses (80-90 Credits)
- Department-specific required courses
- Must pass ALL core courses with minimum C- grade

### Elective Courses (18-24 Credits)
- Choose from department-approved elective list
- At least 2 electives must be from outside your department
- Can include online courses approved by the department

### Final Year Project (6 Credits)
- Mandatory for all programs
- Must be completed in 7th and 8th semester
- Evaluated by a panel of 3 faculty members
- Must achieve minimum 60% to pass

## 3. Graduation Requirements

To receive a degree, a student must:
1. Complete all required credit hours
2. Maintain a minimum CGPA of **2.00**
3. Pass all core courses (minimum C- grade)
4. Complete the Final Year Project
5. Clear all financial dues
6. Complete community service hours (40 hours minimum)
7. No pending disciplinary cases

## 4. Maximum Duration

| Degree | Normal Duration | Maximum Allowed |
|--------|----------------|----------------|
| Bachelor's (4-year) | 8 semesters | 12 semesters (6 years) |

Students exceeding the maximum duration will be **dismissed** from the program.

## 5. Course Repeat Policy

- Students who receive an **F grade** must repeat the course.
- Students may repeat a course to **improve their grade** (max 2 repeats per course).
- Both old and new grades appear on the transcript, but only the **latest grade** counts toward CGPA.
- Repeated courses are charged at **50% of the regular fee**.

## 6. Transfer Credits

- Credits from other HEC-recognized universities may be transferred.
- Maximum **30 credits** can be transferred.
- Only courses with grade **B or higher** are eligible for transfer.
- Department approval is required for each transferred course.

## 7. Dean's Honor List

Students with a semester GPA of **3.50 or above** (minimum 15 credit hours) are placed on the Dean's Honor List. This is mentioned on the transcript and the student receives a certificate.

## 8. Graduation with Distinction

| CGPA at Graduation | Honor |
|--------------------|-------|
| 3.90 - 4.00 | Summa Cum Laude |
| 3.70 - 3.89 | Magna Cum Laude |
| 3.50 - 3.69 | Cum Laude |
